Nutrition coaching, grounded in your health reports.

Longeviti is a practitioner-led platform where certified nutritionists review your blood work, tracking, and daily logs, and deliver personalized meal and supplement plans through a modern app. We're rebuilding the client-facing platform in 2026.

Join the waitlist ↗

What we're building

Longeviti pairs a practicing nutritionist with a mobile app that makes their guidance actionable day-to-day. The practitioner sees your reports and tracking; you see a plan you can follow without guesswork.

For clients

A plan that adapts to you

Upload blood work and health reports. Your nutritionist reviews them and delivers a weekly plan — meals, supplements, and coaching — that evolves as your health does.

For practitioners

A workflow that scales

A web app for certified nutritionists: client intake, report review, AI-assisted plan authoring, and a clean handoff to the client's mobile app.

Under the hood

Built for practice

Schema-validated plan contracts, offline-first mobile sync, and India-region storage for data residency. Architecture chosen for the problem, not imposed by a playbook.

Early access

We're building the first version with a small group of nutritionists and their clients. If you're a practicing nutritionist interested in using Longeviti with your clients — or a prospective client — we'd love to hear from you.
